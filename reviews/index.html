Audience Testimonials


"1816 is a musical that cannot easily be herded into a single genre. It can make you cry, laugh, and think, and you will never at any point be able to predict which emotion is invoked next. A balance is nonetheless masterfully found through the combination of a multifaceted story with masterful songwriting."

"This was a superb production, with outstanding performances by the 5 actors, a sharp and quickfire script, and above all a wonderful set of emotional, complex songs that pushed the plot along wonderfully. This was a really engaging musical with some excellent humour, intricate character interactions and nice choreography."

"It was absolutely captivating! The musical does an excellent job at introducing the characters and making you care for them with brilliant dialogue and fantastic lyrics. The actors are absolutely outstanding and make it impossible for you to look away."

"Stunning production with beautifully arranged, earworm songs, performed by super talented singers/actors and musicians."
"The perfect balance of tension, humour and emotion."

"This show was superb - the music itself was composed in an exceptional way to match the theme of the early 19th century whilst maintaining the modern aspects that draw and keep a listeners attention, performed by a phenomenal group of musicians. The acting both between and during the musical numbers was gripping, showcasing the important historical characteristics of each of the writers themselves, as well as the relationships between them. Each of the performers showcased powerhouse vocals that shone through the performance space, displaying each of their character's thoughts and troubles with seeming ease. Overall, everyone involved in this production did a fantastic job, and congratulations must be passed on to both the director and the writers for producing a piece of student theatre of such a high calibre."

"Incredibly fresh premise, an engaging plot with some very relatable themes and a brilliantly handpicked cast. The actors understood their characters very well and portrayed their wit as well as their insecurities with precision and passion. The music was amazing, the cast’s voices fit very well together and the lyrics were clever and thoughtful. The costumes, lighting and stage design were simple and effective in immersing the audience in the characters’ world. Beyond being an impressive production from a very talented team of students, this musical does the very important job of nurturing one’s curiosity — about the Romantics, literature at large or, as the characters remind us, why we create."

"As a long time musical theater fan this had everything I couldve wanted. It was funny and dramatic and sad but uplifting and hopeful. Incredible work from the cast and writers. The score is amazing and the lighting was very immersive and connected."

"A romantic and well-balanced score with multiple-part harmonies, accompanied by piano and cellos, beautifully setting the scene for the drama unfolding on stage."
